Technical Approach

Our pipeline follows a structured flow: Data ‚Üí Detection ‚Üí Modeling ‚Üí Recreation ‚Üí Sustainability.

üìÇ Data Collection & Preprocessing

  • Gather datasets of Kolam designs (scanned images, digital drawings, existing repositories).
  • Preprocess: noise removal, binarization, resizing, standardization.

üîç Pattern Recognition & Feature Extraction

  • Use Computer Vision (OpenCV, Scikit-image) for line detection, symmetry identification, curve tracing.
  • Extract geometric features: dots, intersections, loops, symmetry axes, fractal patterns.

üìê Modeling & Design Principle Identification

  • Apply Graph-based algorithms to represent Kolam structures (nodes = dots, edges = strokes).
  • Use ML/DL models (CNNs, Autoencoders) for motif classification and principle extraction (symmetry, repetition, recursion).

üé® Kolam Recreation & Generation

  • Implement Generative Models (GANs / VAEs) or Rule-based algorithms to recreate Kolams.
  • Provide interactive tools for users to customize size, symmetry, and style.

üå± Validation & Sustainability

  • Compare generated Kolams with original ones using similarity metrics and visual validation.
  • Deploy as open-source / web-based tool for artists, cultural preservationists, and educators.
  • Enable digital archives of Kolams and eco-friendly learning without physical chalk or paper.
